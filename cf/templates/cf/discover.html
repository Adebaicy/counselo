Who are we ?

Counseling firm as a matter of fact and like for urgency creates free seminars for awareness for singles to help them know which is important to Marriage. Subsequently we organise same for the married to admonish and garnish their homes.

We make a point of duty to help religious leaders in counseling their members who are at the verge of losing their marriages by rendering free counseling to those who can't afford the charges.

30 couples are yearly picked at random to enjoy free guide and counseling. All these on counseling firm to serve humanity generously.

Mission

We are a full time ministry, to be GOD sent by providing quality- based and skillful solutions that are dashing to conflicts and mismanagement of marriage vows and helping relationships in general; to restore back the joy of happy homes where godly children are raised for our society.

Vision

Our vision is to divinely orchestrates homes back to the rightful path of marriage creator who is GOD through godly Counselling, where man and woman roles are well spelt and same been followed to provide an acceptable and Fulfilling Marriages. In 2017 when GOD said to me , go and stop divorce, He clearly made known that His interest in the union of man and woman for marriage can never be quantified.
Counselling firm is here to uphold the love of the Almighty GOD for marriage through His creation man! We planned to spouses to enjoy their marriage and relationship by helping them ease- of tension and resolve any conflicts in their marriage.

Resources

In other to facilitate an hitch free marriage and beautiful relationship, the principal partner in the person of Mrs Owolabi Tolulope has taken it upon herself been the sole proprietress of Counselling firm to undergo so many series of mentorship, training and reading in other to be well fortified and fit as a marriage counselor amongst which are TMFA USA certification, Felix Funke Adejumo mentorship program etc. These were so as to button down on her divine assignment to humanity.

Methodology
